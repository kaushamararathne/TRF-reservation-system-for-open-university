TRF Reservation System - OUSL

The Open University of Sri Lanka (OUSL) offers comfortable residential facilities for students through its Regional Centers (RC) in Colombo, Kandy, and Matara. These hostels can accommodate over 200 students simultaneously at an affordable daily rate. Students engaged in continuous academic activities at OUSL or those from remote areas can apply for accommodation by submitting an application approved by the relevant academic coordinator.
